THE BEGINNING





























KALVIALAYA


kalvialaya

Kalvialaya is a Non- Profitable organization, duly Registered and managed by Kalvialayam Trust, providing free quality after-
school Educational Support to underprivileged children hailing from Villages.


The Centre is functioning in a remote village named Pilappur, since 2010. Pilappur is situated 65 km away from Chennai and 9
km from Chengalpattu Railway Station. We have sufficient infrastructure containing large classrooms fully equipped with
projectors and computers with provision for further expansion.

Mission

The primary objective of Kalvialaya is to empower the rural students, especially girl children, with job knowledge and
personality development skills.
